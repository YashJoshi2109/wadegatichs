About us

Welcome to Wadegati Cooperative Housing Society

A place where community thrives, and modern living meets tradition. Established with a vision to provide quality housing and a nurturing community environment, Wadegati CHS has been a cornerstone of residential excellence for years.

Our Legacy

Wadegati CHS has a rich history dating back to 1971, when a group of visionary individuals came together to create a haven for families seeking a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity

Redevelopment for the Future

As part of our ongoing commitment to our residents, Wadegati CHS is embarking on an exciting redevelopment project. This transformation will usher in a new era of living, offering modern amenities, eco-friendly features, and improved infrastructure.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for all our residents. We are dedicated to creating a sustainable and inclusive living environment that fosters a sense of belonging, security, and happiness

Our Amenities

Discover a life of convenience and community at Wadegati CHS, where you'll enjoy a fully equipped gym, multipurpose hall for various activities, indoor games, and a spacious party hall for special celebrations

Outdoor Arena

Elevate your outdoor living at Wadegati CHS with beautifully landscaped gardens, a vibrant floral garden, a tranquil senior citizen corner, and a jogging/cycling track for your fitness and relaxation.

Infrastructure

Experience modern living with essential infrastructure at Wadegati CHS, including an inviting arrival plaza, 24/7 CCTV surveillance for security, dedicated emergency exit staircases for safety, and high-speed lifts in tower for convenience.

Parking Space

At Wadegati CHS, we offer ample parking options for your convenience. With space for 60 cars, our parking facilities include multiple-level lift parking, stack parking, and a well-organized 4-level parking structure, ensuring you always find a secure and hassle-free place for your vehicle.
